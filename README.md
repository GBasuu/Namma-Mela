# Namma-Mela 🎭

## Android Theatre Seat Booking App

Namma-Mela is an Android-based theatre seat booking application designed for rural drama theatres and mela events. The app allows users to view cast information, reserve seats, and interact through a fan wall. Additional enhancements such as authentication, waiting lists, and multi-show support were also implemented.

---

## Problem Statement

Village drama theatres and mela events often lack a proper digital booking system. People travel long distances only to find seats unavailable, and there is no organized way to view cast details or reserve seats. Namma-Mela solves this problem by providing a digital theatre seat booking platform with seat reservation, cast display, reviews, and admin controls.
